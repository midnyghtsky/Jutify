

## Jute Bag QR Scanner - Eco-Sustainability Platform

### Overview
A vibrant, mobile-friendly website where consumers can scan QR codes from jute bags to discover their journey - from farm to their hands - while learning about the environmental benefits of choosing jute.

---

### 🏠 Landing Page
- **Hero Section**: Eye-catching headline like "Discover Your Bag's Story" with an animated illustration of a jute bag
- **Quick scan button**: Large, prominent "Scan Your Bag" CTA
- **Live Impact Counter**: Animated statistics showing total bags scanned, carbon saved, and trees equivalent - updates in real-time
- **Why Jute?**: Colorful cards highlighting jute's eco-benefits (biodegradable, carbon-absorbing, supports farmers)

---

### 📸 QR Scanner Page
- **Camera View**: Full-screen camera access for scanning QR codes
- **Visual Guidance**: Frame overlay showing where to position the QR code
- **Fallback Option**: Manual ID entry for users who can't scan
- **Permission Handling**: Friendly prompts if camera access is denied

---

### 📦 Bag Details Page
After scanning, show the complete journey of the jute bag:
- **Product Card**: Bag image, name, and batch number
- **Farm Origin**: Region/country where the jute was grown with a mini-map
- **Artisan Story**: Information about the workers who crafted the bag
- **Sustainability Metrics**: Carbon footprint saved vs plastic, water usage, biodegradability timeline
- **Certifications**: Any eco-certifications (organic, fair trade, etc.)
- **Timeline**: Visual journey from seed to finished product

---

### 🌍 Eco-Education Section
- **Why Choose Jute**: Comparison with plastic bags (with fun animations)
- **Environmental Impact**: Facts about jute cultivation and carbon sequestration
- **The Jute Journey**: How jute is grown, harvested, and processed

---

### 💾 Backend (Lovable Cloud)
- **Jute Bags Table**: Store product info, origin, artisan details, certifications
- **Scan Metrics Table**: Track total scans for the impact counter
- **Sample Data**: Pre-populate with a few demo jute bags to test

---

### Design Approach
- **Color Palette**: Vibrant greens, warm yellows, and natural browns
- **Playful Animations**: Bouncy micro-interactions, animated icons
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for scanning on phones
- **Engaging Typography**: Bold headings, friendly fonts
